Text: A book design for a friend's documentary wedding photography.

*Client:* Randy MacNeil and MJ Michaud
*Brief:* Design and print a book of the photographs I had shot for my friend Randy's wedding. The book should be easily distributable to friends and family and present it as a narrative similar to the way they had planned the wedding and the events around it. The audience for the book was Randy, MJ and their families.
*Format:* 8x10" hardcover with dust jacket, 122p of uncoated 148gsm paper
*Year:* 2014

Hello! I'm Jerome — a web developer from Montreal. I design and build websites, currently with Laravel, Vue.js/Nuxt and Kirby CMS.

I work across the web, from REST APIs to full sites and apps. I love designing too — books especially — and I'm happiest figuring out how things work and building them.

Away from the screen I'm an avid cyclist, musician, reader and photographer. Say hi — don't be shy.

weather is a single-page experiment, as a minimalist weather app — sprocket a cycling workout planner and tracking app using Vue and Nuxt — chrysanthemums an accordion photo book of two trips to Japan, handmade from darkroom prints — book watcher is a Laravel app that tracks book release dates and pings me when their status changes — double project zines four handmade b&w zines, each pairing two related series — darkroom timer a single-page Vue.js timer for film development with savable recipes — the invisible cities my photography website and print shop, with series, a journal, and prints for sale.
